MIDI Captain Firmware

Version {{VERSION}}

This installer will install the MIDI Captain custom firmware and an interactive installer app.

What gets installed:

  • MIDI Captain Installer.app in /Applications/
  • Firmware files in /usr/local/share/midicaptain-firmware/
  • CLI tool "midicaptain-install" in /usr/local/bin/

After installation: The MIDI Captain Installer app will open automatically. Connect your device and click Install!
